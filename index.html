About HomPro

Empowering Builders & Homeowners Since 2010

At HomPro, we believe that every project, big or small, deserves the best foundation. Established with a vision to revolutionize the hardware industry in Zimbabwe, we have grown from a modest tool shop to a premier destination for construction and home improvement solutions.

Our commitment goes beyond just selling products. We partner with top global brands to ensure durability and reliability. Whether you are a large-scale contractor building a skyline or a DIY enthusiast renovating your kitchen, our expert team is dedicated to providing you with the right tools and advice to get the job done right.
